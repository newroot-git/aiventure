The problem

Three reasons nothing ever happens.

It dies in the chat

“We should hang out” rots in the group chat. And if it survives, it’s 40 messages of “who’s in?” and “what time again?” — nobody actually running it.

The scroll ate the plan

“Social” media quietly swapped living for watching — hours a day on everyone else’s life instead of going and having your own.

You’re out of ideas

The same three spots on repeat — or no clue what to do at all. With the crew, or just you on a quiet Sunday.

So we built AIventure.

Tell it roughly what you’re after. It comes back with real, grounded things to do — often somewhere you’d never have found on your own. Share one link and your crew’s in. Works for six people or just you; a Tuesday coffee or a two-week trip. Then it keeps the memory. No feed. No performing. No admin.
