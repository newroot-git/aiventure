The idea

Less scrolling.
More living.

The anti-social-media app: no feed, no followers. It gets you and your crew out the door — then remembers it for you.

Getting in

One tap in.

No download to join a plan. Judges: hit “I'm a judge” for an instant guest pass — no email, straight into the demo.

Step 1 · Pick the scale

From a coffee to a trip.

A surprise, one activity, a full day back-to-back, or a multi-day trip away. One engine, any size.

Step 2 · Describe it

Just say it plainly.

Type it like you'd text a mate — “a hike then a pint Saturday.” Drop a place with real autocomplete, anywhere on earth.

Step 3 · AI does the legwork

It grounds every idea.

The agent comes back with real, bookable places — never made-up venues. The time, the spot and the logistics, sorted.

Step 4 · A real plan appears

Real spots. Crew votes.

Each step gets real options with Maps links to prove they exist. The crew votes; the top pick wins. Tweak it in plain English anytime.

Step 5 · Lock it in

Pick a day. Done.

Set the date, see the route mapped, and add it to everyone's calendar in one tap. No 200-message thread, no chasing.

Step 6 · Counting down

Your next one, up top.

The next adventure sits front and centre with a live countdown — not buried under a feed of other people's lives.

It just shows up

Every plan, on its day.

Locked plans land straight on the calendar and in your real calendar app. The admin handles itself.

Step 7 · The payoff

A life, logged.

Finish it and it becomes an Adventure card you keep. A growing record of things you actually did — not a feed of things you watched.
